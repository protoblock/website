What is Protoblock?

Protoblock is a blockchain community driven by NFL statistics and a new consensus algorithm named Proof of Skill. Protoblock was founded in 2014 and has been active since the 2014 NFL season. Protoblock offers an inherently-fair and decentralized platform for playing Fantasy Football.
