Projections

A fantasy projection is like a prediction on how many fantasy points a player will score. The projection can be between 1 and 40 points. All users with projections on a player are eligible to receive a portion os the Fantasybits that were generated by that player. Those with more accurate projection will receive a larger award of fantasybits, based on teh distriution algorithim. Anyone can make projections on 1 player or all 700 player each week on the NFL season. Projections are free, all that is needed is a fantasy name.
