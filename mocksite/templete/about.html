Our Story

ProtoBlock was born form our passion for fantasy football and our software development expertise. The story begins when our founder, Jay Berg, joined the programmers fantasy football auction draft in 1999 while working at Bloomberg L.P. headquarters on Park Avenue, NY. Our founder, Jay Berg, We believe fantasy football is a skill, and professional veterans are professionals, in the category of entertainers and celebrities. Fast forward 20 years to 2008 and the invention of the blockchain, and the revelation that value can be created merely via consensus of peers. Professional Rotisserie Football players, can now be rewarded with real value, simply by demonstrating their skills, knowledge and expertise. The value is bestowed on them by the 50 million amateur players, who use this data to make better picks in their fantasy football games.
