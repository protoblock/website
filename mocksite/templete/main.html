How to play

Traders play Protoblock Football by making projections on NFL player’s fantasy results. Traders are able to make projections for every offensive player - QB, RB, WR, TE, K. Based on the accuracy of their projections, Traders are rewarded tokens in the form of FantasyBits and are recognized on the official Protoblock Football leaderboard. Traders can then choose to use FantasyBits to trade NFL players on Protoblock Football’s gamified exchange, buying when a player is undervalued and selling when a player is overvalued in attempt to profit and earn more FantasyBits. The act of buying and selling with profit and loss creates an efficient market, which generates each NFL player’s price that reflects all relevant information. Player prices, stats and charts generated from Protoblock Football’s Traders are displayed in a streaming FantasyTicker that users can follow to receive the most accurate, market-driven intel in the fantasy football industry. FantasyBits are rare, with 100 FantasyBits minted for every fantasy point scored each week by NFL players. FantasyBits can only be earned with raw fantasy football skills, which is determined by Protoblock Football’s groundbreaking “Proof-of-Skill” consensus algorithm. FantasyBits act solely as tokens on the Protoblock Football platform and cannot be traded for real currency.
