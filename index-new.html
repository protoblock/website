Whether you ride the pine or are a Fantasy Football Expert, Protoblock allows you to use your knowledge of NFL Football to earn cryptocurrencies and prestige.

Fantasybits are Protoblock’s tokenized version of fantasy football skill, they are minted through professional sports statistics and distributed to players according to their ability to predict results.

Our exclusive Proof-of-Skill consensus algorithm makes Protoblock players not only winners, but owners in our immutable, public ledger.

FantasyBit

The Fantasy Football Cryptocurrency

A.K.A. The PPR Coin

Level 1

Generation

During the Regular NFL Season, each PPR pooint generates 100 FantasyBits.

Level 2

Distribution

After each game, internal smart contracts distribute FantasyBits to users based on the accuracy of their projections.

Level 3

Utility

Players can use FantasyBits as margin to bet or hedge on or against NFL players' fantasy results.
