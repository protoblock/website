Projections

Projections are quite simple. In the Protoblock application one just says what they think a player is going to score for a week (PPR). So say that you think that Devonta Freeman will score 20 points (PPR). Then that (20 points) would be your projection. One can also take a look at other protoblock players and use there projections that they have set. After the game is over is when the fantasy bits are awarded to you. Think of it as a way to gain fantasy bits with out having to trade. If you do not know protoblocks scoring system please click the button below to learn more.

Details

Lets us take a look at a Devante Freeman from 2015. As you can see we have posted his results below. You can click on week to change the week for 2015. In the table there are five coloumns Week,Fantasy Name,Projection,Result and Award. Here is what they are.

  • Week

    This is the current week that you are viewing for 2015

  • Fantasy Name

    This is the other protoblock players that played in 2015

  • Projection

    What the protoblock player thought that Devante Freeman would score for the week that you are viewing

  • Results

    The acculaul results for Devante Freeman for the week that is in focus

  • Award

    What the protoblock player was awarded for his projection on Devante Freeman for the week that is in view

As you can see the person's that are the closest to the correct overall result get awarded more. This makes it so that everyone can have a say on what they think Freeman or any other nfl player is actually worth.
