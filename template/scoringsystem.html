Protoblock Scoring System

Offence
Passing Yards 0.5 points per yard
Passing TD 4 points
Passing Interceptions -1 point
Rushing Yards .1 point per yard
Rushing TD 6 points
Reciving Yards .1 point per yard
Reciving TD 6 points
Reception 1 point per reception
Two point conversion 2 points per passer,runner or catcher
Kicking
PAT Kick 1 point
Field Goal 3 points for 1-30 yards, .1 point for each yard after
Defence
Sack 1 point
Takeaway (int,fumble recovery,special teams takeaway) 2 points
Defensive TD 6 points
Saftey 5 points
Shutout 12 points
1-6 points allowed 10 points
7-10 points allowed 8 points
